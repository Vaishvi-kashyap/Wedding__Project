Couple – Aditya and Parita
Venue – Taj, Chandigarh
Good wedding photographers have talent and what it takes to create a good picture but the best wedding photographers have all that in abundance and more. They have vision, are disciplined and are monuments of patience. No wonder then that Vintage Flower Weddings Photographers has some of the best wedding photographers in the landscape.

Marriages are said to be ‘made in heaven’ but the best wedding photographers in the business showed that a slice of heaven could be brought down to earth at the commonplace Taj, Chandigarh, where the couple Aditya and Parita decided to bind themselves in matrimony. That it was a Hindu wedding didn’t faze our highly professional team, which is well and truly inducted into the nuances of the diverse wedding rituals and traditions of all regions, be it pre-wedding shoots or contemporary style wedding photography.

The team at Vintage Flower Weddings Photographers is not classified as one of the best wedding photographers for nothing. They have the ability to capture the magical moments and craft a dream of a story that happens only once in a lifetime. They love their work and enjoy it so much that Aditya and Parita had only kudos and gratitude for turning their very special day into something unique and for being able to preserve the laughter, the chaos, the tears, the happiness and all the varied emotions that were at play.

Vintage Flower Weddings Photographers has some of the best wedding photographers for pre-wedding shoots. The days before the wedding were no doubt chaotic for Aditya and Parita but were also filled with good cheer and celebrations. Our team managed to capture the excitement, the magic, the antics and the cheerful bonhomie of two families coming together. Capturing those unrehearsed moments, the laughter and joy is a precursor to the happier event—in fact it was a sort of stress buster that left Aditya and Parita in a magical daze.

If our team outdid themselves at the pre-wedding shoots, they excelled at the actual wedding. An artistic touch here and there, hint of lighting at the right time and right place, admirable sense of vision coupled with realism created a portfolio that was resplendent, magical and yet sobering. They outclassed even their previous best performances.
